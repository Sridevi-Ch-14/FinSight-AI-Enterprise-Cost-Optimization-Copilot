## 🎯 Core Features Implemented

### 1. Vendor Spend Intelligence
Upload company spend data and get instant analytics across vendors, departments, and categories.

### 2. Vendor Consolidation Radar
Automatically detect overlapping tools (Zoom + Teams + Meet) and calculate consolidation savings.

### 3. SaaS Subscription Optimization
Identify over-provisioned licenses and premium tier downgrade opportunities.

### 4. Shadow IT Detection
Flag unauthorized vendor purchases by individual departments.

### 5. Contract Renewal Management
90-day visibility into upcoming renewals with auto-renew tracking.

### 6. Price Benchmarking
Compare your pricing against market rates and identify overpaying scenarios.

### 7. Alternative Recommendations
Get feature-parity alternatives with zero quality loss (e.g., Datadog → Grafana).

### 8. Finance Copilot
Ask questions in natural language: "Top 5 vendors by spend?" and get SQL + results.

### 9. Executive Dashboard
CFO-ready visualizations with spend breakdowns and savings opportunities.

### 10. Savings Simulation
Calculate total potential savings across all recommendations.
